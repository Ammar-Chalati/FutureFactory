Visas

Study Visa

University admission in the best universities in Germany

Visa 16 is a two-year visa that gives the student sufficient time to obtain the visa and prepare himself with the appropriate language and appropriate university admission before starting the study, which can be in German or English.

Upon obtaining the visa, the student is given from the embassy a residence permit for a period of 3 months only to reach Germany. Upon arrival, the residence permit must be renewed and the papers must be fully prepared in order for the student to obtain a residence permit for a period of two years.

To obtain a visa, the student must have a B1 language level or a B1 level determination in the German language from the Goethe Institute (this applies to all students except for the Syrian student who lives in Syria because there is no Goethe Institute in Syria). Or the TOEFL level in English, which is It is necessary in order for us to start with all the procedures necessary to prepare the papers

The beginning of what a student needs to obtain a study seat in Germany.

Every student, whether a university study or a master's study, needs a university admission + language acceptance + a closed bank account (938.30 * 12 euros) renewed every year in order to extend the visa or instead of having a sponsor for the student in Germany.

The language level required in all German universities for the student to start his studies is the C1 level, meaning that the student must finish levels A1, A2, B1, B2, C1 before the first of October of each year, which is the start date of the first semester for most branches and majors in German universities.

And because the date for submitting applications for conditional admission in all of Germany is set until 15/5 in order to obtain admission on 15/7 or until 15/12 in order to obtain conditional admission on 15/2 and it is conditional on language level B1, so we seek to secure University admission with language acceptance from universities that do not have a specific time for submitting applications and are not conditional on language, in order for the student to come to Germany as soon as possible, regardless of his level in the German language.

As for opening a bank account, we need a copy of the passport only in order to open a closed account for the student within 3 days, and then the student transfers the required amount according to the type of visa, whether a study visa (8650 * 12 euros) or a job search visa and within 3 days he gets from The bank will confirm the necessary confirmation to the embassy in order to obtain the visa.
